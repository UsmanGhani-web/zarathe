Licensed Therapist Position

We're looking for compassionate, licensed therapists to provide online therapy through our secure platform. Help us transform mental healthcare.

Job Overview

As a Zaratherapist, you'll provide individual therapy sessions to clients through our secure video platform. You'll have the flexibility to set your own schedule while being part of a supportive community of mental health professionals.

Location

100% Remote • Work from anywhere

Flexibility

Set your own hours • Full or part-time

Support

Clinical supervision • Peer consultation

Compensation

Competitive pay • Monthly bonuses

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide individual therapy sessions via secure video platform
  • Develop personalized treatment plans for clients
  • Maintain timely and accurate clinical documentation
  • Participate in regular clinical supervision and training
  • Collaborate with our care coordination team as needed
  • Adhere to all ethical guidelines and licensure requirements

Requirements

  • Master's or Doctorate degree in Counseling, Psychology, Social Work, or related field
  • Active, independent clinical license (LPC, LMFT, LCSW, PsyD, or equivalent)
  • 2+ years of post-licensure clinical experience
  • Experience with evidence-based therapies (CBT, DBT, ACT, etc.)
  • Comfort with technology and telehealth platforms
  • Commitment to cultural competence and inclusive care
